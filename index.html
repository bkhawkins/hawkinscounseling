Meet B. Keith Hawkins, DCCC

As a Licensed Clinical Pastoral Counselor with the NCCA, I am dedicated to providing a safe, confidential, and professional environment for individuals and families to navigate life's challenges.

My approach is grounded in the belief that every person is uniquely created. By integrating proven clinical temperament therapy with sound Biblical principles, we work together to identify the root causes of distress and develop a path toward lasting restoration.
